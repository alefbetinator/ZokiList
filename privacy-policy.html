Privacy Policy for ZokiList

Last Updated: May 7, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how ZokiList ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our task management application ("the App").

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide you with a functional task management experience:

  • User Name: We collect the name you enter to create your personalized task list. This is used to identify your tasks in our database.
  • Task Data: We store your tasks, including titles, descriptions, completion status, categories, and due dates.
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app functionality and troubleshooting.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase: We use Firebase to store and sync your task data. Firebase collects certain information as described in their Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Services: Standard iOS services may collect information according to Apple's privacy policy.

Data Usage

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining the core functionality of the app
  • Synchronizing your tasks across devices when using the same user name
  • Improving the app based on usage patterns
  • Fixing bugs and technical issues

Data Storage and Security

Your task data is stored in Firebase Firestore database with appropriate security rules. Your user identification is stored securely in your device's Keychain. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, but no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your data by using the logout function in the app and then contacting us. This will remove all your tasks from our database.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
